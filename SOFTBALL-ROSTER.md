## Weekly Workflow (Once Phone System is Set Up)

### Monday Morning
1. **Send availability check** (SMS to all players)
   - "Hey [Name], available for softball Wednesday night? Reply YES or NO"
2. Track responses in weekly availability log

### Monday Evening
1. **Follow up** with players who haven't responded
   - Call or text reminder

### Tuesday
1. **Confirm roster** - ensure we have enough players
2. If short on players:
   - Contact backup/sub list
   - Alert Chris if we're still short

### Wednesday Morning
1. **Send game reminder** to confirmed players
   - Time, location, any updates
2. Final availability check for anyone uncertain

### Post-Game
1. Update player stats/notes if needed
2. Mark any no-shows or last-minute cancellations
