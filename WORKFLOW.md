## 📋 Daily Routine

**Morning (7:00 AM):**
- You get morning briefing via Telegram (weather + AI dev news)
- Quick check: anything urgent for today?

**Throughout the day:**
- Message me tasks, ideas, reminders as they come up
- I'll capture them in TODO.md or CALENDAR.md
- Ask me "what's on my TODO?" or "what's coming up?" anytime

**Evening (optional):**
- Quick review: "What did I accomplish today?"
- Plan tomorrow: "What should I focus on tomorrow?"

---

## 🗂️ File System

**TODO.md** - Your active task list
- Today/This Week (urgent stuff)
- Upcoming/Scheduled (future tasks with deadlines)
- Someday/Maybe (ideas, low priority)
- Done (archive of completed tasks)

**CALENDAR.md** - Important dates
- Upcoming events, trips, deadlines
- Regular commitments
- Automated tasks

**MEMORY.md** - Long-term memory (main session only)
- Important context, decisions, lessons learned
- NOT for daily logs (use memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md)

**memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md** - Daily logs
- What happened each day
- Technical notes, session summaries

## 💬 How to Work With Me

**Adding tasks:**
- Just tell me: "Add to TODO: call the dentist tomorrow"
- Or: "Remind me to follow up with Lily about New Orleans in 2 days"

**Checking status:**
- "What's on my TODO?"
- "What's coming up this week?"
- "Did I finish X yet?"

**Reminders:**
- "Remind me about X at [time]"
- "Follow up on Y in [timeframe]"
- I'll create cron jobs for scheduled reminders

**Notes & Context:**
- "Remember that Lily likes X"
- "Note: project Y needs to be done by Z"
- I'll capture this in appropriate files

## 🎯 Keep It Simple

We're starting basic:
- TODO.md for tasks
- CALENDAR.md for dates
- Just talk to me naturally - I'll handle the filing

As you get comfortable, we can add:
- Project tracking (PROJECTS.md)
- Meeting notes (meetings/ folder)
- Client/contact info
- Whatever helps!

**Golden rule:** If you think of it, tell me. I'll make sure we don't forget.
